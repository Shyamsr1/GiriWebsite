Achievements

Starting with the highlight of having given many performances all over India and Abroad. She has performed Classical Carnatic, Tevaram, Thiruppugazh, Harikatha concerts, Music composition and vocal support for Bharathnatyam in many temples all over India and several prestigious sabhas like Sri Krishna Gana Sabha-Chennai, The Music Academy-Chennai, Mylapore Fine Arts-Chennai, Ayodhya Mandapam-Chennai, Nungambakkam Cultural Academy-Chennai, Bharath Kalachar-Chennai, TTD(Tirumala Tiupathi Devasthanam, Chennai), Jameshedpur Rama Bhaktha Samajam, Kolkata Vidwath Samajam, Tanjore Vidwath Samaj, and foreign countries like – Singapore, Japan, W. Germany, Canada, Malaysia, West Indies and Nairobi. Listing some of her notable achievements:

  • Started performing Harikatha from the age of 7. Pudukotai Sri Santhanandha Swamigal gave the title “KALARATHNAM”
  • “A” grade artist in Harikatha in ALL-INDIA RADIO & DOORDARSHAN.
  • Sang several songs composed by Padmavibhusan.Dr.BalamuraliKrishna, Padmasri.Dr.Kunnakkudi Vaidyanathan, etc.
  • Had also acted and sung in a Tamil film “ARANGETRAM” directed by Shri. K. Balachander.
  • Participated in many competitions conducted by Tamizh Isai Sangam and won many prizes in the following music fields such as Thevaram, Thiruppugazh, Tamizh Isai Padalgal, Thiruvachagam, Harikatha, Padavarnam, etc from former Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu Sri. C.N. Annadurai and Sri.Kamarjar (1969 – 1974).
  • Got the “Naavanmai Mikka Nall Esai Selvi” by Kalaimamani ISAIPERARIGNYAR Saidhai Thevaram.T.Natarjan in 1970.
  • Was conferred with the title “Sabhanayakan” by Balaji fine arts in the year 1976 for Harikatha.
  • Composed Music for Karma veerar Kamarajar Dance drama, Bhooloka vaikuntam dance drama, Lord Muruga dance drama and Ahalya dance drama.
  • Won the first place in music competition in the All India NCC (National Cadet Corps). Awarded by Hon. President of India Shri. Neelam Sanjeeva Reddy and was also appreciated by Hon. Prime Minister Smt. Indira Ghandhi.
  • “Nrithya Sangeetha Varshini” title was given by the Sarasalaya (A school of classical Bharatanatyam dance) – 1999.
  • Conferred with the prestigious Tamil Nadu State “Kalaimamani” award in the year 2000.
  • Worked as a Music and Sanskrit teacher in Padma Seshadri Bala Bhavan School-T.nagar,Chennai and has trained several students to participate in many competitions, cultural activities, and events. Her students have won several prizes.
  • Worked as a Lecturer and later became the Principal of Tamil Nadu Government Music college-Chennai.
  • Has been invited as “Chief Guest” in several schools, colleges, arangetram functions, and sabhas all over India.
  • Has been invited as Judge for several carnatic music, tevaram, harikatha, and shloka competitions in many schools, colleges in India.
  • Has been external examiner in several colleges like Queen Marys College – Chennai, Tamil Nadu Govt Music college - Chennai, Kalakshetra-Chennai.
  • “Harikatha Shikhamani” award in 2007 by His Holiness Pujya Shri Kanchi Jayendra Saraswathi Swamigal.
  • "Madhura Gaana Manonmani” Award was given Senkundra Mahajana Kalvi Sangam on 03.10.2009.
  • Received “Madura Murali Puraskar” Award from Padmabushan Dr. M. Balamuralikrishna on the occasion of his 82nd Birth anniversary celebration held during the month of July 2012.
  • Received “Sudharani Ragupathi Endowment” award from Naradha Gaana Sabha trust for Bharatanatyam vocal held during the month of August 2012.
  • Award announced as “Ganakala Sironmani” by “Mannargudi Sambasivayar Trust” for Hairkatha performance, held during the month of December on 9th 2012.
  • “Dharmaathma” award from Vaithyasubramani Iyer trust, Mandavali-Chennai in the year 2013 for Harikatha kalakshepam.
  • Dharma Kesari Solar K.S.Subramanya Iyer Trust awarded “Samskritha Rathna” on 23.November.2013.
  • “Best Teacher Award” from Inner Wheel Club of Madras South in the year 2014.
  • “Vidhwan Madurai Krishna Iyengar Award” from Krishna Gana Sabha for vocal in the field of Bharathanatyam in the year 2014.
  • “Smt. K.J. Sarasa Memorial Award” for vocal support for bharathnatyam in the year 2015 (Feb).
  • “Sathya Sangeetha Sundaram” Award was awarded to her by Bharathaanjali Trust on 15.11.2015.
  • "Harikatha Choodamani" given by Poojyasri Jayendra Saraswathi Swamigal and Vijayendra Saraswathi Swamigal at Krishna Gana Sabha on 09.09.2017.
  • Sri krishna sweets has recognized her contribution in Music with “NALLOR VIRUDU” award in OCT 2018.
  • "GEETHA KALA NIPUNA” award in the year 2019 by “Kala pradarshini”.
  • “THIRUMURAI VITHTHAGAR” award given by “Veda Aagama Deiva Thamizh Isai Mandram” and “SPSKC Trust” in the year 2019 March.
  • “Gaandharva Nipuna” award by “ABHAY” from Hon. Governor of Tamil Nadu Shri. Rosaiyah Reddy in the year 2019 APRIL.
  • "Harikatha Vithagar” award given by “Thyaga Brahma Gana Sabha” and “SPSKC charitable Trust” in MAR 2023.
  • "Sakala Devatha Anugraha Gaana Praveena” award by “Siva Anugraha Trust” by Hon. Ex. Governor of West Bengal Shri.Gopal Krishna Gandhi in the year JUNE 2023.
  • "Women Leadership Award” for Harikatha by “Mayuri Academy” in the MAR 2023.
  • Jai Hind Bharath academy has conferred upon her the prestigious “MS Subbulakshmi” award in SEP 2024.
  • The same “Jai Hind Bharath Academy” has conferred upon her the prestigious “Chiranda Pudumai Penmani-Mahakavi Bharatiyar” award in Year DEC 2024.
  • Was conferred with the honorary “SANGITA VACHASPATI” award given by “SASTRA SATSANGH” on Feb 2025 for her excellence in Harikatha.
